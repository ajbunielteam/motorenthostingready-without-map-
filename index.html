About MOTORENT

MOTORENT is a comprehensive online marketplace platform that connects customers with multiple independent motorcycle rental providers. Our platform serves as a centralized booking system where customers can browse, compare, and book motorcycles from various rental providers, each with their own inventory, pricing, and pickup locations.

For rental providers, MOTORENT offers a complete management system to manage your motorcycle inventory, track bookings, view analytics, and grow your rental business. All rental provider accounts are managed and approved through our Super Admin system to ensure quality and reliability.

Multiple Rental Providers

Browse and compare motorcycles from various independent rental providers all in one platform. Each provider operates independently with their own inventory and pricing.

Easy Search & Filter

Search motorcycles by name, filter by transmission type (Automatic or Manual), and filter by specific rental providers to find exactly what you need.

Simple Booking Process

Book motorcycles in minutes with our streamlined process. Upload your valid ID, select pickup and return dates, and get instant confirmation.

Secure ID Verification

Upload or capture a photo of your valid driver's license or government-issued ID during booking for secure verification and compliance.

Provider Locations

Each rental provider has their own pickup location. View provider profiles to see their location and choose the most convenient option for you.

Rental Provider Dashboard

Rental providers can manage their motorcycle inventory, track ongoing and returned bookings, view analytics, and update their profile through a comprehensive dashboard.

Super Admin Management

Our platform includes a Super Admin system to manage and approve rental provider accounts, ensuring quality service and platform integrity.

Flexible Pricing

Each rental provider sets their own pricing. Pricing varies by provider and rental duration, giving you options to find the best deal for your needs.
